# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may take appropriate action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
