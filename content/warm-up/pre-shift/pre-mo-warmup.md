A 10-minute warm-up sequence designed to prepare your body before heading out on shift. Combines joint maintenance, dynamic stretching, and activation to get your hips, spine, and shoulders moving well.

## The Sequence

### Activation Exercises (3 min)

| Order | Exercise | Duration |
|-------|----------|----------|
| 1 | [Shoulder CARs]({{< relref "/mobility/cars/shoulder-cars" >}}) | 3 circles each direction, each side |
| 2 | [Hip CARs]({{< relref "/mobility/cars/hip-cars" >}}) | 3 circles each direction, each side |
| 3 | [Ankle CARs]({{< relref "/mobility/cars/ankle-cars" >}}) | 3 circles each direction, each side |
| 4 | [Wrist CARs]({{< relref "/mobility/cars/wrist-cars" >}}) | 3 circles each direction, each hand |

### Dynamic Stretches (5 min)

| Order | Exercise | Duration |
|-------|----------|----------|
| 5 | [Cat-Cow]({{< relref "/mobility/spine-and-core/cat-cow" >}}) | 8–10 reps |
| 6 | [Thread The Needle]({{< relref "/mobility/spine-and-core/thread-the-needle" >}}) | 5 reps per side |
| 7 | [Hip Flexor Lunge]({{< relref "/mobility/lower-body/hip-flexor-lunge" >}}) | 30 sec per side |
| 8 | [Leg Swings]({{< relref "/mobility/lower-body/leg-swings" >}}) | 10 per direction, per leg |

### Activation (2 min)

| Order | Exercise | Duration |
|-------|----------|----------|
| 9 | [Glute Bridges]({{< relref "/warm-up/activation-drills/glute-bridges" >}}) | 10 reps |
| 10 | [Bodyweight Squats]({{< relref "/warm-up/activation-drills/bodyweight-squats" >}}) | 10 reps |

{{% notice tip %}}
This routine hits the same body areas you'll load throughout the day — hips, spine, shoulders, and ankles. Do it consistently and you'll feel the difference by mid-shift.
{{% /notice %}}
