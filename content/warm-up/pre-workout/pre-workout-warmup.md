A 12–15 minute warm-up sequence to prepare for strength training, conditioning, or any high-intensity session. Progresses from joint prep to dynamic mobility to muscle activation.

## The Sequence

### Joint Prep (3 min)

| Order | Exercise | Duration |
|-------|----------|----------|
| 1 | [Shoulder CARs]({{< relref "/mobility/cars/shoulder-cars" >}}) | 3 circles each direction, each side |
| 2 | [Hip CARs]({{< relref "/mobility/cars/hip-cars" >}}) | 3 circles each direction, each side |
| 3 | [Ankle CARs]({{< relref "/mobility/cars/ankle-cars" >}}) | 3 circles each direction, each side |

### Dynamic Mobility (5 min)

| Order | Exercise | Duration |
|-------|----------|----------|
| 4 | [Leg Swings]({{< relref "/mobility/lower-body/leg-swings" >}}) | 10 per direction, per leg |
| 5 | [Hip Flexor Lunge]({{< relref "/mobility/lower-body/hip-flexor-lunge" >}}) | 30 sec per side |
| 6 | [Lunge With Spinal Twist]({{< relref "/mobility/lower-body/lunge-with-spinal-twist" >}}) | 5 per side |
| 7 | [Walking Quad Stretch]({{< relref "/mobility/lower-body/walking-quad-stretch" >}}) | 5 per side |
| 8 | [Standing Arm Swings]({{< relref "/mobility/spine-and-core/standing-arm-swings" >}}) | 15 reps |

### Activation (4 min)

| Order | Exercise | Duration |
|-------|----------|----------|
| 9 | [Glute Bridges]({{< relref "/warm-up/activation-drills/glute-bridges" >}}) | 10 reps |
| 10 | [Banded Side Steps]({{< relref "/warm-up/activation-drills/banded-side-steps" >}}) | 10 steps each direction |
| 11 | [Good Mornings]({{< relref "/warm-up/activation-drills/good-mornings" >}}) | 10 reps |
| 12 | [Bodyweight Squats]({{< relref "/warm-up/activation-drills/bodyweight-squats" >}}) | 10 reps |

{{% notice tip %}}
After this general warm-up, add 1–2 warm-up sets of your first working exercise at lighter weight before jumping to working sets.
{{% /notice %}}
